COMPANY OVEREVIEW

In these unpredictable times, the transport industry, more than any other, undergoes several transformations in its business strategy, processes and methods to stay relevant to consumer needs. It has witnessed more sophistication in infrastructure and logistics, holding efficiency and sustainability as the big picture. Keeping this in view, digitalization of freight brokerage market stands pivotal to growing business. LorryCircle, an evolving digital platform and a marketplace, functioning with innovative business models, aiming to offer safe and effective domestic road transportation services.

We operate across India and USA, providing multi-model access to customers, service providers, agents, truck fleet owners, vendors, drivers and more. Our skilled team of technical and non -technical professionals are dedicated to offering unique solutions to transport needs. We address issues related to technology, analytics and automation thereby ensuring exemplary experience of NextGen logistic operations. LorryCircle unites various stakeholders in the field of transportation, to move forward and grow together.
